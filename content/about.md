My name is Di Pan, a Master's student in Physics at **École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)**, working in the Fields & Strings Laboratory under [Prof. João Penedones](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/fields/). I completed my Bachelor's at **Tsinghua University** under Prof. Wei Song.

I am fascinated by the *quantum origin of spacetime and gravity*. My current work focuses on lattice Villain Hamiltonians with boundaries, and on quantum corrections to black hole partition functions. I am also broadly interested in holography, generalized symmetries, and the role of quantum information in understanding spacetime.

Outside of physics, I write small things in the rain, sort Pinyin in JavaScript, and maintain a blog called *The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya*.
