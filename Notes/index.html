Notes

A working archive of reading notes — quantum gravity, holography, and the maths I keep forgetting. These are rough and may contain errors; email corrections are welcome.

2025

  • Schwarzian action, briefly — How the Schwarzian derivative emerges as the boundary mode of nearly-AdS₂ gravity (JT gravity, bulk dilaton, reparametrisation mode). [Quantum Gravity]
  • Replica trick & wormholes — Re-deriving the entropy formula from gravitational replicas; the dominant saddle becomes a connected wormhole, giving the QES prescription from a Euclidean path integral. [Holography]
  • Modular Hamiltonians I can remember — Why the modular Hamiltonian for a half-space is the boost generator (Bisognano–Wichmann), and generalisations to spherical regions in CFT. [Quantum Info]
  • On the JT path integral — Two takes on the matrix-model dual of JT gravity: the Saad–Shenker–Stanford genus expansion and the Mertens–Turiaci approach via Liouville. [Quantum Gravity]

2024

  • Conformal blocks for the impatient — Global block from SL(2,ℝ) representation theory; Virasoro block via Zamolodchikov recursion; heavy-light limit. [CFT]
  • Edge modes & the area term — Why gravitational entropy is geometric: boundary degrees of freedom at the entangling surface contribute the area term. [GR]
  • Extremal Kerr — what's so special — Near-horizon geometry as an AdS₂ fibration; SL(2,ℝ)×U(1) isometry enhancement; the Kerr/CFT chiral CFT. [Quantum Gravity]
  • Subregion duality, three ways — Entanglement wedge reconstruction via Petz map, modular flow, and the JLMS relation. [Quantum Info]
  • Cohomology, but for the kitchen — An attempt to explain de Rham cohomology via cooking metaphors. Imperfect but stuck. [Math]
